1. Introduction

Welcome to Cropio's Privacy Policy. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our file conversion service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Name, profile picture (optional)
  • Contact Information: Email address for communication
  • Payment Information: For premium subscriptions (processed securely by third-party payment processors)

2.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect information about how you use our Service:

  • Conversion History: Types of conversions performed, file formats, timestamps
  • Usage Statistics: Number of conversions, storage used, features accessed
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, pages viewed, actions taken

2.3 File Information

When you upload files for conversion:

  • File Metadata: File name, size, format, upload timestamp
  • File Content: Temporarily stored for processing (automatically deleted after 30 minutes)
  • Conversion Results: Output file metadata and conversion status

2.4 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze usage patterns and improve our Service
  • Provide security features

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 To Provide the Service

  • Process your file conversions
  • Manage your account and subscriptions
  • Provide customer support
  • Send service-related notifications

3.2 To Improve the Service

  • Analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Develop new features and improvements
  • Monitor and improve conversion quality
  • Optimize performance and user experience

3.3 For Security and Compliance

  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and property

3.4 For Communication

  • Send account-related emails (verification, password reset)
  • Notify you of service updates and changes
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Send promotional emails (only with your consent)

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.2 Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in:

  • Cloud hosting and storage (AWS, Google Cloud, etc.)
  • Payment processing (Razorpay, Stripe, etc.)
  • Email delivery services
  • Analytics and monitoring tools

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for specified purposes.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:

  • Valid legal requests (court orders, subpoenas)
  • Government investigations
  • Protection of our rights and safety
  • Prevention of fraud or illegal activities

4.4 Business Transfers

If Cropio is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Data Storage

  • Account Data: Stored securely in encrypted databases
  • Uploaded Files: Temporarily stored during processing, automatically deleted after 30 minutes
  • Conversion History: Stored for your reference (metadata only, not file content)
  • Location: Data is stored on secure servers in compliance with applicable laws

5.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest
  • Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Firewall and intrusion detection systems

5.3 File Security

  • Files are processed in isolated environments
  • Automatic deletion after 30 minutes
  • No permanent storage of file content
  • Malware scanning on uploads

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Update

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Update or correct your account information
  • View your conversion history
  • Download your data

6.2 Delete Your Account

You can delete your account at any time through your account settings. Upon deletion:

  • Your account information will be permanently deleted
  • Your conversion history will be removed
  • Any uploaded files will be immediately deleted
  • This action cannot be undone

6.3 Marketing Communications

You can opt out of promotional emails by:

  • Clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any promotional email
  • Updating your email preferences in account settings
  • Contacting us directly

Note: You will still receive essential service-related emails (account verification, password reset, etc.)

6.4 Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of the Service.

7. Data Retention

7.1 Account Data

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service.

7.2 Uploaded Files

All uploaded and converted files are automatically deleted after 30 minutes for privacy and security.

7.3 Conversion History

We retain conversion metadata (file names, formats, timestamps) for your reference and service improvement.

7.4 Deleted Accounts

After account deletion, we may retain certain information for:

  • Legal compliance (tax records, transaction history)
  • Fraud prevention and security
  • Dispute resolution

Retained data is anonymized where possible and deleted when no longer needed.

8. Children's Privacy

Cropio is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and that your data receives adequate protection.

10. Third-Party Links

Our Service may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

11. GDPR Compliance (For EU Users)

If you are located in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR:

11.1 Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract: Processing necessary to provide the Service
  • Consent: Marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Legitimate Interest: Service improvement, security, fraud prevention

11.2 Your GDPR Rights

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain types of processing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

11.3 Data Protection Officer

For GDPR-related inquiries, contact our Data Protection Officer at: privacy@cropio.com

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how we use it
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending you an email notification (for material changes)

Your continued use of the Service after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

14. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@cropio.com
  • Support Email: support@cropio.com
  • Website: www.cropio.com

15. Your Consent

By using Cropio, you consent to the collection, use, and sharing of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.
